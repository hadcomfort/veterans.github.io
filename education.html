Veterans Education & Training Resources

Explore official resources for education benefits, career guidance, and training programs for veterans and their families.

Official Education & Training Resources

The following are external links to official government resources. You will be navigating away from this website.

  • VA Education and Training Benefits

    The main portal for U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs education and training benefits. Learn about the GI Bill, eligibility, how to apply, and other educational programs.

  • GI Bill Comparison Tool

    This VA tool helps veterans compare benefits by school, calculate tuition assistance, and find information on Yellow Ribbon Program participating institutions.

  • Veteran Readiness and Employment (VR&E)

    Formerly known as Vocational Rehabilitation and Employment, this VA program (Chapter 31) assists veterans with service-connected disabilities to prepare for, find, and maintain suitable employment.

  • Free Educational and Career Counseling (Chapter 36)

    VA's Personalized Career Planning and Guidance program offers free educational and career counseling to transitioning service members, veterans, and eligible dependents.

  • Dependency and Indemnity Compensation (DIC)

    A tax-free monetary benefit generally payable to eligible survivors (spouse, child, or parent) of service members who died in the line of duty or veterans whose death resulted from a service-related injury or disease. This can help fund education for dependents.
