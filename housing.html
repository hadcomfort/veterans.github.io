Veterans Housing Assistance Resources

Explore official resources for VA home loans, housing grants, and support programs for homeless or at-risk veterans.

Official Housing Assistance Resources

The following are external links to official government resources. You will be navigating away from this website.

  • VA Housing Assistance

    The main portal for U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs housing assistance programs, including home loans, grants for veterans with disabilities, and help for homeless veterans.

  • VA Home Loans

    Learn about VA direct and VA-backed home loans to help veterans, service members, and surviving spouses buy, build, improve, or refinance a home.

  • Housing Grants for Disabled Veterans

    Information on VA housing grants for veterans and service members with certain service-connected disabilities to buy, build, or modify a home to meet their needs.

  • Homeless Veterans Resources (VA)

    Access VA programs and resources aimed at helping homeless veterans or those at risk of homelessness. Includes information on the National Call Center for Homeless Veterans.

  • HUD-VASH Program

    The Housing and Urban Development-Veterans Affairs Supportive Housing (HUD-VASH) program combines HUD housing vouchers with VA supportive services to help homeless veterans and their families find and sustain permanent housing.
