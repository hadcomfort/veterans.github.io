Veterans Healthcare Resources

Explore official resources for healthcare services, benefits, and support for veterans.

Official Healthcare Resources

The following are external links to official government resources. You will be navigating away from this website.

  • VA Health Care

    The main portal for U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs health care services. Find information on eligibility, how to apply, covered services, and managing your health and benefits.

  • Find a VA Location

    Use this tool to find VA medical centers, outpatient clinics, Vet Centers, and other VA facilities near you.

  • Veterans Crisis Line

    Confidential support for Veterans in crisis and their families and friends. Available 24/7 by phone, text, or online chat.

  • VA Mental Health Services

    Provides information and resources for a wide range of mental health conditions, treatment options, and support services offered by the VA.

  • Foreign Medical Program (FMP)

    For U.S. veterans living or traveling abroad, the FMP helps cover medical care for service-connected conditions.

Eligibility for VA Health Care

  • Eligibility for VA Health Care

    Understand who qualifies for VA health care benefits and how priority groups affect access and costs.

How to Apply for VA Health Care

  • How to Apply for VA Health Care

    Learn about the application process, what documents you'll need, and how to apply online, by phone, by mail, or in person.

Covered Services

  • Covered Services

    Explore the range of services covered by VA health care, including primary care, specialist appointments, mental health services, home health care, and prescription medications.

VA Community Care

  • VA Community Care

    Find out if you're eligible to receive care from community providers when VA care is not readily accessible.

Health Needs and Conditions

  • Health Needs and Conditions

    Information on care for specific needs like women's health, and resources for service-related conditions such as those related to Agent Orange or Camp Lejeune water contamination.

Manage Your Health Online

  • Manage Your Health Online

    Access VA tools to refill prescriptions, schedule appointments, and communicate with your healthcare team.
