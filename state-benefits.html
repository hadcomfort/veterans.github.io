State-Administered Benefits Overview

Many essential veteran benefits, such as property tax exemptions, educational assistance, and special employment programs, are administered at the state or territory level. These can vary significantly from one state to another. To find the resources relevant to you, it's important to connect with your local state department of veterans affairs.

Find Your State's Department of Veterans Affairs

The National Association of State Directors of Veterans Affairs (NASDVA) provides a central directory to help you locate your state or territory's official veterans affairs department. Please visit their resources page to find the contact information and website for your specific location:

  • NASDVA Resources - State Directors Directory

Once on the NASDVA site, click on your state or territory to be redirected to its official page, where you can find detailed information about local benefits and services.
