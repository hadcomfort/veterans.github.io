Veterans Employment Resources

Explore official resources for job searching, career training, and employment rights for veterans.

Official Employment Resources

The following are external links to official government resources. You will be navigating away from this website.

  • DOL VETS Main Page

    The U.S. Department of Labor's Veterans' Employment and Training Service (VETS) provides an overview of employment services and resources available to veterans.

  • Find a Job (CareerOneStop via DOL)

    This DOL VETS page offers resources for job searching, including links to CareerOneStop, American Job Centers, and tools to match military skills to civilian careers.

  • HIRE Vets Medallion Program

    The HIRE Vets Medallion Award is a federal program recognizing employers for their commitment to veteran hiring, retention, and professional development.

  • Transition Assistance Program (TAP - DOL)

    The Department of Labor's portion of TAP provides information, tools, and training to help transitioning service members and their spouses prepare for civilian employment.

  • Uniformed Services Employment and Reemployment Rights Act (USERRA)

    USERRA protects the employment rights of individuals who voluntarily or involuntarily leave employment positions to undertake military service or certain types of service in the National Disaster Medical System.

  • VA Careers and Employment

    The U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs offers resources for veterans seeking employment with the VA and other federal agencies, including information on Veteran Readiness and Employment (VR&E) services.
